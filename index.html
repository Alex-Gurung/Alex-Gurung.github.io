Project Codet

I am a curriculum coordinator at Project Codet, a computer science Non-Profit that aims to teach and inspire future computer science students. Aside from running small workshops, I helped run and created the curriculums for the major event, called "Codet Day", where hundreds of elementary to middle school students were helped in creating projects related to different areas of computer science. This years projects were related to Fashion, Biology, and Gaming, chosen because they showcase the wide use of computer science.
