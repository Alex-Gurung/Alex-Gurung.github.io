Range Function

When I'm writing programs I often find myself needing a random number within a range(e.g. a random year from 2000 to 2016). However, I often forget the syntax or method, and even if I do remember I can't remember the correct implementation. To solve this problem I made a simple, bare-bones, website that, when given a miminum and maximum value, will output the code required for your desired language(Java, Python, or C++)
